# SECURITY AND ETHICS FOR SYNC

## Sensitive Data

This app may contain names, contact details, life history, emotional content, family information, and consultation notes. Treat exported and synced data as sensitive.

## Cloud Consent

Before enabling cloud sync for real records, confirm the client understands where records are stored and who can access them.

## High-Risk Situations

This system is not a replacement for emergency, medical, legal, or crisis intervention. Do not rely only on the app for:

- Self-harm or suicide risk.
- Domestic violence or immediate safety risk.
- Acute psychiatric or medical symptoms.
- Child or elder protection concerns.
- Legal reporting obligations.

Use appropriate referral, emergency, or mandated reporting procedures.

## Access Control

Use individual login accounts. Do not share one account across unrelated practitioners.

## Export Control

Exports may contain sensitive consultation material. Store them encrypted when possible and delete unnecessary copies.

## Sync Risk

Cloud sync adds convenience but also adds risk. The formal sync mode must show sync state clearly and avoid silent overwrites.
